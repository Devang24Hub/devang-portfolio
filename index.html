About Me

I design with intent,
build with craft.

I'm Devang Bhattad, a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Developer based in Jawhar, Maharashtra. I live at the intersection of beautiful design and clean code — creating products that are not just functional, but genuinely delightful to use.

My workflow starts with deep user empathy — understanding real problems before touching Figma or a keyboard. From wireframe to polished prototype to pixel-perfect implementation, I own the full design-to-dev pipeline.

I believe every button, every animation, every micro-interaction is an opportunity to make someone's day a little better. That obsession with detail is what separates good design from great design.
